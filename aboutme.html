Is This Thing On?

Hi, I'm Adam. I was born and grew-up in West Bend, WI, a small city about 40 miles north of Milwaukee. Most of my adolescence was spent in the countryside of the Kettle Morraine and as such I've always had a deep appreciation for the nature and wildlife. Now I find myself living in Milwaukee as I attend devCodeCamp with the hopes of forging a better future for myself. With most of my time spent indoors and focused on the glow of a laptop I still enjoy, and find I really need, to go out and see the sky. It helps me to focus on my next task and keeps me centered. Little habits like this are the things I do to try and keep myself on-track and able to learn in an environment I was not the most familiar with.
